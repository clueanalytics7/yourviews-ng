Data Privacy & Compliance

NDPR Compliance: In accordance with the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR), YourViews is committed to protecting the personal data of our users. We implement technical and organizational measures to ensure a level of security appropriate to the risk.

Data Minimization: We only collect the minimum information necessary for policy analysis. All demographic data is optional and used solely for aggregated statistical purposes.

Anonymity: Votes are linked to browser fingerprints to prevent duplication, but we do not store personally identifiable information (PII) like names or phone numbers.
