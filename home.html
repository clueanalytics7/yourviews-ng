Data Protection & NDPR Compliance

YourViews strictly adheres to the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR). We implement robust technical and organizational measures to ensure the security, confidentiality, and integrity of all data collected through our platform.

Our "Privacy by Design" approach ensures that we minimize data collection to only what is necessary for demographic analysis. We do not sell or share individual-level data with third parties for commercial purposes.

  • Encrypted transmission of all user feedback.
  • No collection of PII (Personally Identifiable Information).
  • Regular audits to ensure NITDA regulatory alignment.

By using this platform, you consent to the processing of your anonymous demographic data for community development research.
