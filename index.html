About YourViews

Our Mission

Empowering local citizens to participate actively in democratic municipal review cycles. We aggregate local voices into quantitative data structures to aid community organizations and policy strategists.

How It Works

Policies and proposals are listed straight from municipal councils and direct community requests. Once you cast a vote, your response is validated, logged anonymously, and dynamically calculated into live visual analytics.

Legal Compliance

We preserve your structural rights to anonymized security. System designs strictly adhere to Nigeria Data Protection Regulations (NDPR) using temporary client tracking instead of gathering directly personal data.
